Biography

Groundworkz Construction specializes in commercial and residential decorative concrete.  I use the very best colors, stamps, concrete, and techniques to create beautiful sidewalks, porches, patios, steps and driveways.  My goal is to communicate well with clients and help you design and create unique hardscapes.   I believe in “old fashion” materials and workmanship and still use a compacted rock base and rebar throughout all of my projects.  Many contractors have done away with these very basic and critical steps.  Decorative concrete is a very strong and beautiful surface that will give you years of low maintenance use.  Let Groundworkz Construction help you create a one of kind project with the strength of concrete and the beauty of stone, pavers, bricks, wood and more.
